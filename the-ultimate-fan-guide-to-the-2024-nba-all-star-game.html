The Ultimate Fan Guide to the 2024 NBA All-Star Game

April 2024 · 4 minute read

By Fan Arch February 16, 2024 04:34

Photo by Kyle Terada-USA TODAY Sports

The 73rd NBA All-Star Game is set to take place in Indianapolis from February 16-18, 2024. This comprehensive guide will provide you with all the information you need to make the most out of this exciting weekend.

Event Overview

The 2024 NBA All-Star Game is bringing back the classic East-West matchup and traditional scoring system. The weekend will kick off with the Celebrity Game and Rising Stars Challenge on Friday, followed by the NBA All-Star practice, HBCU Classic, and All-Star Saturday Night, which includes the Skills Challenge, NBA All-Star 3-Point Contest, Stephen Curry vs. Sabrina Ionescu 3-Point Contest, and the Dunk Contest. The weekend will culminate with the 73rd NBA All-Star Game on Sunday, pitting the Eastern and Western Conference All-Stars against each other .

All-Star Game Starters and Reserves

The starters for the All-Star Game were determined through a voting process involving fans, current players, and a media panel. The voting accounted for 50 percent of the total vote, with the remaining 50 percent split between current players and the media panel. Notably, there will be no All-Star draft this year, and each conference's leading vote-getter will serve as the captain. The leading vote-getters are LeBron James for the East and Giannis Antetokounmpo for the West.

The All-Star reserves were selected by the 30 NBA coaches. Additionally, injury replacements were named for Julius Randle and Joel Embiid, with Scottie Barnes and Trae Young taking their places. The complete rosters for both conferences have been finalized, ensuring an exciting lineup of talent for the All-Star Game.

Event Venues

The main venues for the NBA All-Star Game Weekend events include Gainbridge Fieldhouse, Lucas Oil Stadium, and the Indiana Convention Center. Gainbridge Fieldhouse will host the Rising Stars Game, Practice & Media Day, HBCU Classic, and the All-Star Game. Lucas Oil Stadium will be retrofitted to accommodate the Ruffles Celebrity Game, State Farm All-Star Saturday, and NBA Crossover Concert Series. The Indiana Convention Center will feature the NBA Crossover fan experience and NBA Crossover Concert Series.

Schedule of Events

The All-Star Weekend will feature a variety of engaging events, including the Celebrity Game, Rising Stars Challenge, All-Star practice, HBCU Classic, Skills Challenge, 3-Point Contest, Stephen vs. Sabrina Challenge, Dunk Contest, and the 73rd NBA All-Star Game. These events are spread out across the weekend, ensuring non-stop entertainment for basketball fans.

Social Impact and Community Involvement

The NBA's commitment to social impact will be on display in Indianapolis during the All-Star 2024 festivities. The league has named the Special Olympics of Indiana and Boys & Girls Clubs of Indianapolis as the charity beneficiaries for the All-Star Game. Moreover, through the NBA All-Star 2024 Rewards Program, fans have the opportunity to win tickets, merchandise, and other prizes by supporting local businesses in Indiana.

Fan Experience and Prohibited Items

The NBA All-Star Weekend provides an interactive fan experience featuring player appearances, live performances, and activities around art, music, fashion, technology, and entertainment. Fans can download the “NBA Events” app and check in at participating local businesses and iconic basketball landmarks across the state to participate in the rewards program. However, there are certain prohibited items for the All-Star Game Weekend events, including weapons, large bags, large flags, offensive signs and clothing, drones, and other item.

Player Performances and Entertainment

The 2024 NBA All-Star Weekend will feature performances by several artists, including Jennifer Hudson, as well as headline performances by Babyface. Additionally, NBA star Stephen Curry and WNBA star Sabrina Ionescu are set to participate in a 3-Point Contest, adding another layer of excitement to the weekend's events.

The 2024 NBA All-Star Game in Indianapolis promises to be an electrifying weekend filled with basketball action, engaging fan experiences, and impactful community involvement. With a star-studded lineup of players and a diverse range of events, the All-Star Weekend is set to captivate fans and offer an unforgettable experience for basketball enthusiasts.
