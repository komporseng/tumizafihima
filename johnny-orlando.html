Biography

Johnny Orlando was born in Mississauga, Canada on January 24, 2003. Gained a following by posting covers of pop songs by artists such as Austin Mahone and Justin Bieber to his YouTube channel, oftentimes alongside his older sister Darian. He is known for such original songs as “What If” and “Missing You.” In 2018, he was cast to star in Total Eclipse. He collaborated with Mackenzie Ziegler on the 2016 song “Day and Night.” He started making videos when he was 8 years old and Darian was 13. He and Darian began by creating videos for original songs titled “Summertime,” “Replay,” and “Never Give Up.”
