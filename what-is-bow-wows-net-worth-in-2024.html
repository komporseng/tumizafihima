Shad Gregory Moss, professionally known as Bow Wow, is a multi-talented American rapper, actor, and television host.

He gained early recognition in the music industry, releasing successful albums at a young age, and has since diversified his career into acting, investment, and entrepreneurial ventures.

As of April 2024, Bow Wow's net worth is estimated to be approximately $1.5 million.

Early Career and Music Success

Bow Wow's career took off at the age of 13 with the release of his first album, "Beware of Dog," in 2000, followed by "Doggy Bag" in 2001.

These albums contributed significantly to his initial success in the music industry, with record sales of over 3 million copies for "Beware of Dog" by 2009 and over 1.1 million copies for "Doggy Bag" in the US alone.

His subsequent albums, including "Unleashed" in 2003 and "Wanted" in 2005, further solidified his reputation as a successful rapper, with several hit songs topping various Billboard charts.

These achievements, along with his compilation album "The Very Best of Bow Wow" in 2010, contributed to his rising net worth.

Financial Ventures and Business Engagements

In addition to his music career, Bow Wow has ventured into various business endeavors that have impacted his net worth.

Notably, he has delved into acting, securing roles in both television and film, including appearances in "CSI: Cyber," "F9: The Fast Saga," and "Madea's Big Happy Family."

He has also expanded his influence into the fashion industry, partnering with KISS Colors & Care as a celebrity ambassador and previously co-owning a prestigious shoe store in Atlanta. 

Financial Challenges and Legal Issues

Despite his successes, Bow Wow has faced financial challenges and legal issues that have influenced his net worth.

In 2012, he was ordered by a court to pay $3,000 a month in child support, and in the same year, he claimed to be making only $4,000 a month with minimal funds in his checking account.

2019 was a tough year for Bow Wow, as he was arrested for battery after a fight with a woman, leading to legal repercussions.

While he has encountered financial and legal challenges, his achievements in the entertainment industry and entrepreneurial pursuits have contributed to a net worth of approximately $1.5 million. 

Despite fluctuations in his net worth, his contributions to the music and entertainment industry have solidified his position as a prominent figure, with potential for further financial growth in the future.
