Quinn Hughes

Quinn Hughes, the eldest of the Hughes brothers, is a defenseman for the Vancouver Canucks in the National Hockey League (NHL). Known for his exceptional skating ability and offensive prowess, Quinn has quickly become a force to be reckoned with in the league. His vision and playmaking skills are remarkable, as evidenced by his ability to control the pace of the game and create scoring opportunities for his team. Jack Hughes

The middle brother, Jack Hughes, is a center for the New Jersey Devils. Jack was the first overall pick in the 2019 NHL Entry Draft, a testament to his incredible talent and potential. Luke Hughes

The youngest of the Hughes brothers, Luke Hughes, is an up-and-coming defenseman who is projected to be a top pick in the 2021 NHL Entry Draft. Luke possesses a rare combination of size, skill, and hockey IQ, making him an exciting prospect for NHL teams. He is a smooth skater with excellent offensive instincts, much like his older brother Quinn. While Luke is yet to make his mark at the professional level, his potential is undeniable, and he has already impressed scouts and analysts with his performance in junior leagues.
