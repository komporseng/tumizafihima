The Thompson Twins: Story of How Twins Conquered the NBA Against All Odds

May 2024 · 3 minute read

The tale of Amen and Ausar Thompson, the first brothers taken in the top five picks of the 2024 NBA Draft, is a feel-good story for the ages. The twins grew up in a basketball family and have followed identical paths to the NBA. Here, we break it down and get into what it was like to be brothers growing up and playing basketball, sidelining with Overtime Elite, and making history as the first brothers ever to be first-round selections in the NBA Draft.


The Early Days and Sibling Battles


The Thompson twins, alongside their older brother Troy, were brought up in the game of basketball whilst growing up in Oakland, California. Troy, their older brother, had done much of what had shaped them — Troy was always around, always encouraging, always pushing them to be the most competitive they can. He was always on the field and offering mentorship, a point of constancy in an inconsistent childhood.


The Impact of Family and Mentorship

Troy's impact on the basketball life of the twins was strong. He not just inspired an affection for the amusement in them, but additionally turned into a guide out of potential pitfalls and on a way of duty to ball, family, and instruction. This is an influence that has, at the very least, been instrumental in creating the twins' beautifully formed yet almost militaristic dedication to what they do.


The Overtime Elite Experience

Amen, and Ausar both developed their talents at Overtime Elite; an institution with major credibility in the production of high-level basketball talent. The time they spent at Overtime Elite boosted their profile as basketball players and made them known, which is why they were able to make history in the NBA Draft.


NBA Draft Accomplishment


Amen and Ausar Thompson made history when the Houston Rockets selected Amen with the fourth pick and moments later the Detroit Pistons chose his brother Ausar with the fifth pick. That accomplishment certainly put them on the elite basketball radar, as it was a monumental achievement and a testament to their ability with a basketball in their hands.


Brotherhood and Connected Dreams

From a young age, it was clear that the Thompson twins were nothing if not committed to their basketball dream. The Daily Beast had a feature on the boys and their grueling daily workouts from the time they were young — a portrait of determination and hard work. The two of them have been fraught with obstacles and roadblocks, but their combined goal of being the all-time best NBA players that they can be has molded them into the men that we see today.


The Overview

The story of Amen and Ausar Thompson is one about the strength and bond of two brothers as they work towards a similar goal. The bond they share and the remarkable success they've had can be traced back to their childhood while they were growing up and playing together as they started their NBA journey.
Certainly, the Thompson twins are exceptional young talent who will contribute a great deal to the future success and expectations surrounding sprinting, but just as important is the impact of support to young athletes. As the Thompson twins enter the grand stage of the NBA, they bring the values imbibed in them during their childhood which not only make them great athletes but exemplary role models for the budding generation of basketball aspirants.
