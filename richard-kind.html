Richard Kind Biography

Richard Bruce Kind is an American actor and comedian, best known for his roles as Andy in Curb Your Enthusiasm (2002–2021), as Dr. Mark Devanow in Mad About You (1992–1999, 2019), as Arthur in A Serious Man (2009), and Paul Lassiter in Spin City (1996–2002).

Moreover, Kind is also known for his voice performances in various Pixar films such as Inside Out (2015), the first two films of the Cars franchise (2006-2011), Toy Story 3 (2010), and A Bug’s Life (1998). Kind also voices Marty Glouberman in the Netflix animated series Big Mouth (2017-present). He was selected for a Tony Award for Best Featured Actor in a Play for his performance as Marcus Hoff in the 2013 Broadway production of The Big Knife.
