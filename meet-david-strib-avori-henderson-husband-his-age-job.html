David Strib Job

David Strib is a creative director, filmmaker, and photographer by profession. Per his LinkedIn handle, he has been working as a Creative Director at Official Strib since January 2017.

David’s IMDB profile shows that he has worked as a director and editor for a couple of music videos like Jason Derulo & Jawsh 685: Savage Love and Jason Derulo & David Guetta Feat. Nicki Minaj & Willy William: Goodbye and as a director and cinematographer for Jason Derulo: Colors – Lyric Video.

Sharing about his journey to becoming a filmmaker and a photographer, David told that it was actually his passion for acting that led him to film and photography, eventually buying him his first DSLR.

As a photographer, David mainly busies himself with client shoots like working with brands and the public. Apart from that, he also puts tons of time into developing his Instagram account, creating content, and developing further with each post.

Talking about the gears David uses to get those crispy shots, they are Canon 1dx Mark ii, Canon 16-35mm f2.8 L ii, Canon 24- 105mm f4 L, and Lowepro ProTactic 450 AW backpack.

David Strib’s wife, Avori Henderson, is a former beauty pageant model turned gamer, content creator, and now a reality TV personality.
