Is Ryan Pemberton Dead? Who Was Ryan Pemberton? How Did Ryan Pemberton Die?

June 2024 · 2 minute read

The NASCAR community mourns the sudden loss of Ryan Pemberton, a distinguished crew chief in both the Cup and Xfinity Series. Pemberton’s untimely passing at the age of 54 marks a somber period, especially following the recent death of his brother, Randy. This article delves into Pemberton’s impactful career, his role at JR Motorsports, and the touching statement from Kelley Earnhardt Miller.

Who Was Ryan Pemberton?

Ryan Pemberton was a revered figure in NASCAR, known for his strategic acumen and leadership in the pit box. Over a career spanning several decades, he oversaw 637 races, including both the Cup and Xfinity Series. Pemberton’s expertise led to notable successes: two wins in the Cup Series and three in the Xfinity Series. His most significant stints included victories with Joe Nemechek in 2004 and Brian Vickers in 2009 at the Cup level, and achievements with Larry Pearson and Smith in the Xfinity Series. Pemberton’s legacy is not just in his wins but also in the respect he earned from drivers, teams, and the broader NASCAR community.

Pemberton’s Impact at JR Motorsports

In 2012, Pemberton joined JR Motorsports, serving as the director of competition until 2022. His ten-year tenure was marked by significant achievements and growth for the team. Under his guidance, JR Motorsports became a formidable force in the Xfinity Series. Pemberton’s passion for competition and his ability to inspire and lead his team were instrumental in the team’s success. His role extended beyond race strategies, deeply influencing the culture and ethos of JR Motorsports.

Kelley Earnhardt Miller’s Heartfelt Statement

Kelley Earnhardt Miller, a key figure at JR Motorsports, expressed profound grief over Pemberton’s passing. In her statement, she highlighted Pemberton’s crucial role in the team’s achievements and his enduring legacy. She reminisced about his passion, leadership, and the positive impact he had on everyone he worked with. Earnhardt Miller’s words echo the sentiment of the entire NASCAR community, offering condolences to Pemberton’s wife, Andrea, and daughters, Payton and Britton, during this challenging time.

Conclusion

Ryan Pemberton’s sudden passing is a significant loss to the world of motorsports. His contributions to NASCAR, both on and off the track, have left an indelible mark. As the community comes together to remember his legacy, thoughts are with his family and the many lives he touched. Pemberton’s journey in NASCAR, filled with triumphs and challenges, serves as a testament to his dedication and love for the sport. He will be remembered not only for his achievements but also for the character and spirit he brought to the racing world.
