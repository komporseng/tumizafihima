Marta Kostyuk Family Life: Who Are Her Parents?

Marta was born to her Ukrainian parents, Oleh Kostyuk and Talina Beiko. She also has a sister named Mariya Kostyuk. Mariya is a collegiate tennis player who competed for Chicago State University and Southeast Missouri State University.

Marta’s mother Talina is a former professional tennis player (Source: Instagram)

Her mother, Talina is a retired tennis player who represented Ukraine at the national level. Talina won a $10K title in her home city of Kyiv in 1994 and also reached a career-high WTA ranking of No. 391.

Marta’s father, Oleh was the technical director of the Antey Cup. It is a junior tennis tournament in Kyiv. She also has an uncle named Taras Beyko who is a retired tennis player.

Likewise, Marta is a cousin of professional football players Myroslav Slavov and Vadym Slavov.
