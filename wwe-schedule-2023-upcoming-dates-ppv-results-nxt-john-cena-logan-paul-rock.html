WWE schedule 2023: Every upcoming PPV date, NXT Network exclusive and full results including Survivo

July 2024 · 5 minute read

WWE are gearing up for a dramatic end to 2023 after CM Punk made an incredible return.

Fourteen-time world champion Triple H now has full creative control of the company for the first time having taken over from his father-in-law Vince McMahon last year.

Here’s the full schedule for all of the upcoming WWE PPVs…

Results

  • Women's WarGames match - Bianca Belair, Charlotte Flair, Shotzi, and Becky Lynch def. Damage CTRL (Bayley, Asuka, Iyo Sky, and Kairi Sane)
  • Santos Escobar def. Dragon Lee
